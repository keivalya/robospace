Built by roboticists, for roboticists

We've spent years fighting with ROS dependencies, waiting for real robot resets, and explaining why our experiments aren't reproducible. There had to be a better way.

The problem we're solving

Every robotics researcher knows the pain: 3 days to set up a simulation environment, another week to get reinforcement learning working, and good luck sharing your setup with anyone else. Meanwhile, web developers deploy entire applications in seconds.

We asked ourselves: why can't robot training be that simple?

Our approach

RoboSpace brings modern developer experience to robotics. Upload your robot model like you'd deploy to Vercel. Configure training like you'd set up a GitHub Action. Share results like you'd share a CodePen.

No CUDA installation. No ROS melodic vs noetic debates. Just robotics.
